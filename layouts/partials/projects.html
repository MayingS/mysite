Projects

Open Vision Platform for Smart City Intersections
2019.01 - 2019.05, CMU IMIL lab & Zensors Co., Ltd
Research on methods to analyze the presence or absence of particular objects, counts of objects, and activities in the outdoor and indoor scenes in the real world, in Pytorch.
Implemented Occlusion-Net to localize 2D/3D keypoints for vehicles and persons with severe occlusions.
Developed the architecture for detection model training with partially labeled data, by disabling the RPN generated anchors outside of the labeled region.

Safe City with Intelligent Surveillance Technology
2016.05 - 2016.07, Digital China Group Co., Ltd
Designed and implemented algorithms using C++ and OpenCV to detect people gathering in public areas by calculating the distribution entropy of crowd, and to detect sudden scattering of a crowd by analyzing the motions.
Published a patent ‘Group anomaly detection method based on a dense optical flow histogram’ (CN105389567A).
